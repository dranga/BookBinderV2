About BookBinder

BookBinder is a web application to generate custom PDF eBooks from PDF sections.

Features include:
Front cover page and choice of blank back to front cover
Back cover page
Even the number of pages to have the back cover as an even page (like a book)
Add table of contents
Pages are numbered in the right corner for odd pages and in the left corner for even pages
Page number location is adjustable

This is acheived by generating and compiling a LaTeX file. The resulting PDF is returned.
This project is stored on GitHub.
